Agentic Clinical Viva

Overview

Agentic Clinical Viva is an experimental assessment framework where a student's clinical case presentation is evaluated simultaneously by two leading AI models (ChatGPT and Gemini). This side-by-side viva explores:

  • Clinical Localization: The models' ability to identify the anatomical site of the lesion from history alone.
  • Physical Examination: Critical appraisal of the examination findings and their correlation with the suspected etiology.
  • Textbook Alignment: Each round is scored and referenced against standard medical texts like Kundu and Alagappan.

Supervised by Anupam Singh MD, this platform highlights the comparative strengths of AI in medical education and clinical reasoning.

  • The Progressive Paralysis: A Compressive Myelopathy Viva

    A 47-year-old labourer presents with 4 months of progressive back pain, paraplegia, and bladder dysfunction. This agentic viva pits ChatGPT against Gemini in a multi-round evaluation of clinical localization, etiology, and physical examination findings, unmasking the diagnostic milestones of Pott's spine in a resource-limited setting.

  • The Yellowing Tide: A Decompensated Liver Viva

    A 47-year-old daily-wage labourer presents with 2 months of progressive jaundice and abdominal distension. An agentic exploration of chronic liver disease where the AI models probe for etiological clues (alcohol vs. viral), quantify risk, and evaluate the clinical signs of portal hypertension and hepatic decompensation.

  • The Tapping Apex: A Rheumatic Mitral Stenosis Viva

    A 42-year-old homemaker presents with progressive breathlessness, palpitations, and pedal edema. This agentic viva evaluates the AI models' ability to localise the valvular lesion, assess its severity, and identify complications like atrial fibrillation and pulmonary hypertension based on clinical examination findings.

  • The Ascitic Enigma: A Diagnostic Dilemma of Jaundice and Distension

    A 69-year-old female with diabetes presents with chronic jaundice and progressive abdominal distension. This viva probes the complex overlap between chronic liver disease, tuberculous peritonitis, and peritoneal carcinomatosis, highlighting the AI models' role in navigating empirical therapy and exudative vs. transudative logic.
