Medical & Statistical Tools

Anupam Singh, MD is a Professor in the Department of Medicine at Santosh Medical College, Ghaziabad. Beyond clinical practice, he is dedicated to developing open-source tools for Statistical Analysis and Medical Education, aiming to bridge the gap between complex data and clinical utility.
